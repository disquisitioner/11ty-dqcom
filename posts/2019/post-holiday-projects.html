  • Learning how to build a web site with the Eleventy static site generator
  • Taking SmugMug for a test drive to see if it's a better way to share my photos than Flickr
  • Component assembly for my remote home weather sensor, which involves combining a solar panel, power converter, Adafruit Feather M0 Radio, and assorted sensors in a weather-proof enclosure.
  • Reading the wonderful books I got for Christmas
  • Exploring the Sony a6400 camera I rented to see if it'd be worth upgrading from my beloved a6000
  • Playing the electric guitar, given I received both the guitar and amplifier as gifts. (I've not had an electric guitar since 10th grade, so cover your ears.)
